# 📊 Task 4: Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation

---

## 📖 Description
This project focuses on converting data analysis into meaningful business insights and validating findings using basic statistical concepts.

The Superstore dataset was used to analyze sales trends across categories, regions, and time.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand sales performance
- Identify key trends and patterns
- Support business decision-making

---

## 📊 Data Story

### 🔍 Analysis
- Technology category has the highest sales
- West region performs better compared to other regions
- Sales increase over time and peak during November and December

### 📌 Conclusion
- Sales are seasonal and increase towards year-end
- Technology products contribute the most to revenue

### 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing categories like Technology
- Increase marketing efforts during peak months
- Expand business in high-performing regions

---

## 📈 Hypothesis Testing

### Hypothesis
H0: Sales do not increase in November and December  
H1: Sales increase in November and December  

### Analysis
From the dashboard, sales values are higher in November and December compared to other months.

### Conclusion
We reject the null hypothesis and conclude that sales increase during year-end.
